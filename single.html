Darussalam Islamic University, true champions of academic excellence and professional responsibility, is distinguished by its commitment to inestimable value and quality through a focus on innovation and holistic development. It has engraved its name and legacy in the annals of Kerala's educational resurgence, well proportionally incorporating religious and modern learning spheres.

       College of Tharqiyathul Huffaz is an initiative of Darussalam Islamic university, which leaped into the forefront by aptly lending distinctive focus on Qur'anic related studies along with modern education. Admission to Tharqiya' is only for students who have completely memorised the holy Qur'an and do not exceed the age of 14. We provide exceptional teaching and practical learning to inspire and empower our students for personal and professional success. Our goal is to mould scholarly talents, who can successfully sharpen their spiritual and intellectual potential to outclass the challenges of time.

       Tharqiya' is the main attraction of Darussalam Edu Village', a multi-faceted educational project of Darussalam. At present, 300 hundred students have been studying under three categories as Ihdadiya', 'Sanaviya' and 'Aaliya'. One of the peculiar features of our course is that students could complete at least five 'douras' in a single year and forty ' douras' in eight years.

       Our students outfit 'Ahsas' paves the way for nourishment of parliamentary, organisational and political skills and awareness among the students. The campus is always vibrant with varied kinds of programmes and activities, which enable students to channalise their career into a world of unparalleled and unique success.
There are more than 50 hitz colleges accredited by Darussalam Islamic University. Students from these affiliated institutions will be enrolled directly, while others will have to face an entrance exam for admission.

ABOUT DARUSSALAM

Darussalam Islamic University is a reputed educational centre in south India providing both secular and Islamic education forstudents. The courses offered by Darussalam Islamic University include Quran, theology, Islamic jurisprudence, Fiqh, Hadith, history, culture, and so on. Special efforts have been made by DIU for Da’wa activities. DIU emphasizes preparation of studies and educational consultation required to servedifferent sectors of the community. It insists on collection of specialized human resources and requalification making them able for intellectual communication and spiritual renaissance. The main motive behind it was to procreate eminent Islamic scholars, linguists, and orators who can take up the challenges of thetime, solve the problems of the modern world and lead the community through the right path. - DARUSSALAM ISLAMIC UNIVERSITY
